Help us shape our new menu layout

Download our new theme and help us shape the new layout of Aetherhub. Aside from upgrading to a new version we also want to overhaul the whole layout, making it quicker to access on both mobile and desktop. We want to make a toploaded navigation on desktop and make the whole site more streamlined by centering the content and splitting it into two columns.

You can download the theme below, feel free to use it in your own projects. If you are experienced in web developement, html/css/javascript or have worked with Bootstrap 4 we would love input on the new theme. Any suggestions on how to improve the code or other tidbits are much welcome. DM me on Reddit or join our Discord Server and leave your feedback in the #aetherhub-chat channel.
